# Partner Ecosystem Program

**Phase 10 — Market Expansion & Revenue Scaling**  
**Partner types, onboarding, and revenue model.**

---

## Partner types

| Type | Description | Role |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Reseller** | Sells AISTROYKA to end customers; may bundle with services. | Lead gen, deal close, first-line support. |
| **Referral** | Refers leads; no implementation or ongoing management. | Lead gen only; revenue share on conversion. |
| **Implementation / SI** | Implements and configures for enterprise accounts. | Onboarding, training, industry packs, integration. |
| **Technology** | Integrates product (e.g. ERP, accounting, BI). | Integration pathway; co-sell or co-market. |

**Onboarding:** One path per type; certification and revenue share differ by type.

---

## Onboarding model

1. **Apply:** Partner applies (form or direct); provide company, contact, type, and region.
2. **Agreement:** Sign partner agreement (NDA, referral/reseller/SI terms, revenue share).
3. **Enablement:** Access to partner portal (or doc share): product training, demo environment, sales and technical docs, pricing and discount rules.
4. **Certification (if applicable):** Complete certification tier (see below) to unlock benefits.
5. **Go-live:** Referral link or deal reg; first deal support; ongoing communication.

**Portal:** Document what partners need (training, demo tenant, deal reg form, MDF request); build or use lightweight (Notion, shared drive) until dedicated portal.

---

## Certification tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Registered** | Signed agreement; completed basic product training. | Referral link; standard revenue share; access to sales kit. |
| **Certified** | Passed product and sales certification; 1+ closed deal or 2+ qualified leads. | Higher revenue share; deal reg protection; co-marketing eligibility. |
| **Premier** | Sustained pipeline and revenue; implementation or tech integration delivered. | Best revenue share; priority support; joint GTM and case study. |

**Recertification:** Annual refresh (product update, compliance); tier can change based on performance.

---

## Revenue share

- **Referral:** X% of first-year subscription (e.g. 10–15%) on referred customer; paid when customer pays. Cap or clawback if churn within N months (e.g. 90 days).
- **Reseller:** Margin or rev share on subscription (e.g. 20–30%); reseller invoices customer or we invoice and pay partner. Define who holds billing relationship.
- **SI/Implementation:** Typically customer pays SI separately; we may offer referral share if SI brings deal. Optional: SI gets discount for implementation services sold with subscription.
- **Technology:** Referral or rev share on referred revenue; or no rev share and mutual lead exchange. Document per partner.

**Rules:** Revenue share paid quarterly or monthly; minimum payout threshold; partner must be in good standing (no abuse, compliant with brand).

---

## Integration pathways

- **API:** Public or partner API for read/write (projects, tasks, reports) with API key and scopes. Document in developer docs; rate limits and SLAs.
- **Webhooks:** Outbound events (e.g. report.submitted, task.assigned) for ERP or BI. Same auth and security as platform.
- **Export:** Audit log export, report export (CSV/Excel) for accounting or BI. Already scoped in compliance; expose to partner or end customer by role.
- **SSO/SAML:** Enterprise feature; partner or customer IT can integrate identity. Document setup for SI partners.

**No custom code in core:** Integrations use existing APIs and webhooks; partners build connectors.

---

## Co-marketing model

- **Content:** Joint blog, webinar, or case study; we provide product and quote; partner provides customer story and distribution.
- **Events:** Partner includes us in their event (booth, session); we include partner in ours when relevant.
- **Lead sharing:** Co-registered webinars or campaigns; leads split or attributed; follow MDF or co-fund rules.
- **Brand:** Partner uses approved logo and messaging; we list partner on website (partner page) when certified.

**MDF (Marketing Development Funds):** Optional; partner requests funds for campaign; we approve and track; receipt and attribution required.
