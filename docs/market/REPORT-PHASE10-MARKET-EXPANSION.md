# Report — Phase 10: Market Expansion & Revenue Scaling (MAX)

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Role:** Principal Growth Strategist + Market Expansion Architect  
**Project:** AISTROYKA

---

## Executive summary

Phase 10 prepared AISTROYKA for **multi-region, multi-industry, revenue-scaling** growth. All deliverables are **documentation and playbooks** in `docs/market/`: market expansion strategy, localization, industry solution packs, partner program, sales enablement, marketing engine, regional pricing, revenue operations, expansion analytics, and global launch playbook. No core domain rewrites; expansion reuses platform capabilities. Every artifact is executable by operations and growth teams.

---

## Expansion readiness

- **Strategy:** Target regions (home, EU, NA, LATAM, APAC) prioritized; entry sequencing and positioning defined. Competitive landscape and differentiation pillars documented. Ready to execute first expansion region once pilot and scale are validated.
- **Localization:** UI language roadmap (current ru/en/es/it; add de, fr, pt); i18n architecture and locale-aware formatting; translation and legal adaptation documented. New locales can be added per roadmap without domain change.
- **Launch:** Global launch playbook with phased rollout, per-region checklist, PR, partner coordination, and pilot-to-public transition. Reusable for each new region.

---

## Revenue readiness

- **Pricing:** Regional pricing (tiers, currency, PPP, partner discounts, enterprise deals) and revenue operations (lifecycle, billing, invoicing, renewal, churn, upsell) documented. Aligned with existing Free/Pro/Enterprise and docs/growth.
- **Sales:** Product pitch, value prop, ROI logic, objections, demo scripts, personas, and funnel stages in SALES_ENABLEMENT. Demo and sales kit (Phase 8) extended for full sales enablement.
- **Analytics:** CAC, LTV, conversion, activation, expansion revenue, partner-driven revenue, and region performance defined in EXPANSION_ANALYTICS. Implementation in BI/CRM; playbook ready.

---

## Partner readiness

- **Program:** Partner types (reseller, referral, SI, technology), onboarding, certification tiers, revenue share, integration pathways, and co-marketing documented in PARTNER_PROGRAM. Agreements and partner portal to be finalized; model is executable.
- **Coordination:** Partner role in launch and pilot-to-public transition in GLOBAL_LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK. No code change; process and ownership defined.

---

## Launch readiness

- **Per region:** Launch checklist (product, legal, marketing, sales, partners, support, launch day) and PR strategy in GLOBAL_LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK. Pilot-to-public transition criteria and steps defined.
- **Dependencies:** First expansion launch depends on home-region pilot success, scale readiness (Phase 9), and completion of localization and legal for that region. No new technical blocker from Phase 10.

---

## Scale risks

- **Execution capacity:** Expansion requires marketing, sales, CS, and partner management. Documented playbooks assume team capacity; under-resourcing delays launch.
- **Localization and legal:** New region requires localized UI and terms; delay or error here blocks GA. Mitigate with clear ownership and checklist.
- **Revenue and attribution:** CAC, LTV, and partner attribution need clean data (CRM, billing, UTM). Incomplete tracking understates or misattributes; implement early.
- **Industry packs:** Packs are content and config; “apply pack” and demo data in product need implementation. Without packs, time-to-value may be longer in new industries.
