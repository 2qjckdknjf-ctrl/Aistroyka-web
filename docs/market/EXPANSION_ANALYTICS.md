# Expansion Analytics

**Phase 10 — Market Expansion & Revenue Scaling**  
**Metrics for growth and revenue scale.**

---

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

- **Definition:** Total sales and marketing spend (paid, content, tools, people) in period / New paying customers in period.
- **By channel:** CAC per channel (organic, paid, partner) = channel spend / customers attributed to channel.
- **Target:** CAC < LTV/3 (or per target payback); improve with conversion and retention.
- **Track:** Monthly; by region and segment (SMB vs Enterprise).

---

## LTV (Lifetime Value)

- **Definition:** ARPA × gross margin % × (1 / monthly churn). Or sum of revenue over expected lifetime (simplified: ARPA × 12 × expected years).
- **By segment:** LTV for Free→Pro vs direct Pro vs Enterprise.
- **Target:** LTV > 3× CAC; improve with retention and expansion.
- **Track:** Quarterly; refresh churn and ARPA.

---

## Conversion rates

- **Sign-up → Activated:** % of sign-ups that reach activation (e.g. first report or first task assigned) within 7 days. See GROWTH_KPI_FRAMEWORK.
- **Activated → Paid:** % of activated (or trial) that convert to paid in 30/60/90 days.
- **Lead → MQL → SQL → Opportunity → Won:** Per SALES_ENABLEMENT funnel; conversion at each stage.
- **Track:** By source, region, and segment; weekly or monthly.

---

## Activation

- **Manager activation:** % of managers with ≥1 task assigned and ≥1 report reviewed within 7 days. See PRODUCT_ANALYTICS_PLAN and GROWTH_KPI_FRAMEWORK.
- **Worker activation:** % of workers with ≥1 report submitted within 7 days.
- **Tenant activation:** % of tenants with at least one full loop (assign → report → review) in first 14 days.
- **Track:** Per cohort (sign-up week); trend over time.

---

## Expansion revenue

- **Definition:** Revenue from existing customers in period (upgrade, add seats, add-on) minus churn and downgrade.
- **Net revenue retention:** (Starting ARR + Expansion − Churn − Downgrade) / Starting ARR. Target > 100% for healthy SaaS.
- **Track:** Monthly or quarterly; by segment and region.

---

## Partner-driven revenue

- **Definition:** Revenue from customers attributed to partner (referral link or deal reg).
- **Metrics:** Partner-sourced pipeline; partner-sourced revenue; % of new revenue from partners.
- **By partner:** Revenue per partner; tier and performance for partner program.
- **Track:** Monthly; attribute in CRM or billing.

---

## Region performance

- **Metrics per region:** Sign-ups, activated, paid customers, MRR/ARR, churn, CAC, LTV. Compare to target and prior period.
- **Leaderboard:** Which regions are ahead on pipeline and revenue; which need focus (conversion, retention, or investment).
- **Track:** Monthly; review in expansion and leadership meetings.

---

## Dashboards and reporting

- **Weekly:** Pipeline and conversion (lead→won); activation and conversion (sign-up→paid); incidents and support volume.
- **Monthly:** MRR/ARR; churn; NRR; CAC; LTV; partner revenue; region breakdown.
- **Quarterly:** Full funnel and unit economics; roadmap and capacity for next quarter.

**Data source:** Product events (activation); CRM (pipeline); billing (revenue, churn); marketing (spend, attribution). Centralize in BI or spreadsheet; no PII in analytics.
