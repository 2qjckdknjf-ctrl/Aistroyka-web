# Global Launch Playbook

**Phase 10 — Market Expansion & Revenue Scaling**  
**Phased region rollout and launch execution.**

---

## Phased region rollout

| Phase | Region | Activities |
|-------|--------|------------|
| **0** | Home | Prove PMF; pilots and first paying customers; case studies and references. |
| **1** | First expansion (e.g. EU) | Locales and legal (GDPR, terms); 2–3 design partners or pilots; then GA; marketing and partner enablement. |
| **2** | Second expansion (e.g. NA) | Same playbook; payment and legal for NA; partners or direct; GA and demand gen. |
| **3** | Next regions (e.g. LATAM, APAC) | Per MARKET_EXPANSION_STRATEGY; localize and legal; partner-led where possible; GA. |

**Rule:** One region at a time for GA; no parallel “big bang” multi-region launch. Reuse platform; only GTM and ops scale.

---

## Launch checklist (per region)

- [ ] **Product:** Locales and strings; pricing and currency; payment method and tax (VAT/GST). No core domain change.
- [ ] **Legal:** Terms and Privacy for jurisdiction; data residency stated; contracts and DPA if required.
- [ ] **Marketing:** Website and landing pages; SEO and paid for region; lead capture and attribution.
- [ ] **Sales:** Playbook and demo; pricing and discount rules; funnel and CRM for region.
- [ ] **Partners:** Partner agreement and enablement; referral or reseller live; co-marketing if applicable.
- [ ] **Support:** Language and hours; SLA and escalation; KB and FAQ for region.
- [ ] **Launch day:** PR or announcement (if planned); email to list; partner notification; monitor sign-ups and support.

---

## PR strategy

- **Launch:** Press release or blog post for region GA; key messages = value prop and differentiation; quote from design partner or customer if available.
- **Ongoing:** Product updates; case studies; partner or event news. Distribute via email, social, and partner.
- **Crisis:** Single spokesperson or process; no ad-hoc external comment. See INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAYBOOK if technical.
- **Ownership:** Marketing or leadership; align with legal for any forward-looking or sensitive claim.

---

## Partner coordination

- **Pre-launch:** Brief partners on launch date, messaging, and offer (e.g. pilot or discount). Provide assets (logo, one-pager, demo link).
- **Launch:** Partners can announce same day or within week; use approved messaging. Track partner-sourced sign-ups and pipeline.
- [ ] **Post-launch:** Share results (sign-ups, pipeline); thank partners; gather feedback for next region.

---

## Pilot-to-public transition

- **Criteria:** Pilot success score (see GROWTH_KPI_FRAMEWORK) and reference willingness; no critical bugs; support and docs ready.
- **Steps:** (1) Pilot converts to paid or reference-only. (2) Remove “pilot” label from product or offer in that region. (3) Open self-serve or sales-led sign-up. (4) Marketing and PR for GA.
- **Communication:** Tell pilots “we’re going GA”; offer thank-you (e.g. extended discount or case study participation). Do not sunset pilot support abruptly; transition to standard support.

---

## Execution

- **Owner:** Program or growth lead per region; cross-functional (product, marketing, sales, CS, legal).
- **Cadence:** Weekly during launch prep; daily in launch week; post-launch review within 2 weeks.
- **Document:** Store checklist and results (sign-ups, pipeline, issues) for next region and retrospective.
