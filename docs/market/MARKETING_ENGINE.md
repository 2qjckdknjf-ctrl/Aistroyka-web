# Marketing Engine

**Phase 10 — Market Expansion & Revenue Scaling**  
**Website, content, SEO, paid, leads, and case studies.**

---

## Website structure

- **Home:** Value proposition; who it’s for; key benefits; CTA (Start free / Book demo).
- **Product:** Features (tasks, reports, review, AI, mobile); screenshots or short video; pricing link.
- **Industries:** Pages or sections per industry (Residential, Commercial, GC, Renovation, Development); link to solution packs and use cases.
- **Pricing:** Tiers (Free, Pro, Enterprise); feature comparison; FAQ; CTA.
- **Resources:** Blog, guides, case studies, webinars (when available).
- **Partners:** Partner program summary; apply or contact.
- **Company:** About, contact, careers (if applicable).
- **Legal:** Terms, Privacy, Cookie policy; locale or region-specific where required.

**CTA consistency:** Primary = Start free or Book demo; secondary = Contact sales or See pricing.

---

## Content strategy

- **Pillars:** (1) Field visibility and report workflow. (2) Construction and field-team productivity. (3) AI for construction and quality. (4) Compliance and audit.
- **Formats:** Blog posts, short guides (e.g. “How to run a daily report in 5 minutes”), comparison or “vs” (lightweight, factual), customer stories when available.
- **Cadence:** 2–4 posts per month to start; increase with capacity. Repurpose for email, social, and partner.
- **Ownership:** Who writes, edits, and publishes; review for product and legal accuracy.

---

## SEO pillars

- **Keywords:** Field report software, construction task management, photo report app, construction visibility, daily report construction, etc. By region and language.
- **On-page:** Title, meta description, H1/H2, internal links; no keyword stuffing.
- **Technical:** Fast load; mobile-friendly; clean URLs; locale/hreflang for multi-language.
- **Content:** Answer “how to” and “what is” for core terms; link to product and pricing.
- **Track:** Organic traffic and conversions by landing page and keyword; refine quarterly.

---

## Paid channels

- **Search (e.g. Google):** Brand and category terms (construction report software, field management); geo-target by region; landing page = home, pricing, or demo.
- **Social (e.g. LinkedIn):** Target job titles (project manager, superintendent, operations); creative = value prop and short demo or testimonial.
- **Retargeting:** Visitors who didn’t sign up; remind with benefit or offer (e.g. free pilot).
- **Budget and attribution:** Set monthly budget by channel; UTM and conversion tracking; attribute to channel and campaign in EXPANSION_ANALYTICS.

---

## Lead capture flows

- **Self-serve:** Sign up (email/password or OAuth); no form for Free tier; optional “Company name” and “Role” for segmentation.
- **Demo request:** Form (name, email, company, role, region, message); route to sales or CS; auto-respond with calendar link or next steps.
- **Content gated (optional):** Guide or webinar sign-up in exchange for email; add to nurture sequence.
- **Partner:** Referral link with UTM; lead attributed to partner; same sign-up or demo flow.
- **Storage:** CRM or spreadsheet; sync to email tool for nurture; no PII in repo.

---

## Case study pipeline

- **Source:** Pilots and customers with strong results (review time down, adoption high, quote willing).
- **Process:** (1) Get consent and quote. (2) Draft (problem, solution, results, quote). (3) Legal/brand review. (4) Publish on site and in sales kit. (5) Use in email, paid, and partner.
- **Format:** 1–2 pages; optional video or webinar. Template: Company, industry, challenge, how they use AISTROYKA, results (metrics + quote), CTA.
- **Target:** 1–2 case studies per region or industry per year; refresh when new references available.
